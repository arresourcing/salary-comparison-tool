Salary Comparison app uses Income Tax and NI rates from 6 April 2026 to 5 April 2027. For illustration purposes only.

Car Scheme Salary Sacrifice: assumes a £40,000 EV at 4% BiK (2026/27 rate) — the car is provided by your employer in exchange for a reduced gross salary, saving Income Tax and NI on the sacrificed amount, with only a small BiK charge applied instead. Replaces cash car allowance.

Pension Salary Sacrifice: the saving shown reflects the total Income Tax and NI benefit of making pension contributions via salary sacrifice — comparing take-home with the pension contribution versus receiving the same amount as taxable salary with no pension.

Note for higher rate taxpayers: If your salary falls between £100,000 and £125,140, your Personal Allowance reduces gradually to zero — creating an effective 60% marginal tax rate on earnings in this range. Pension salary sacrifice can be an effective way to reduce income back below £100,000 and recover your full allowance.
